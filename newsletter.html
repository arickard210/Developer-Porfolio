Grow your skills, better your game and build your confidence this summer in the Junior Tour!

The Junior Tour is designed to create a competitive tournament atmosphere locally for the junior golfers that are wanting to compete during the summer and improve their tournament play.
Age divisions will be utilized to make for a fair playing field, assignment to a division will be determined based on the players age at enrollment.

The Junior Tour will consist of seven tournaments each held on Sundays in June and July. Players are not required to play every week but are encouraged to do so to help increase their points accumulated throughout the tour for the chance to play in the Wichita Junior Cup.

After the sixth week, the top four players (based on points) from each division will be invited to play in the championship tournament. The Wichita Junior Cup will be held at Arthur B. Sim Golf Course. A catered lunch will be offered to all participants. The winning junior will receive the Wichita Junior Cup trophy for their age division!

REGISTRATION OPENS APRIL 4!

Wichita Junior Golf Foundation teaches kids, ages 9-18 the rules and basic skills of golf. It is an eight-week summer program held Monday and Tuesday mornings in June and July. The program is offered at the Wichita Public Golf Courses. Two courses are dedicated to beginners.

Boys and girls 9-18 years old are eligible to participate in the Wichita Junior Golf program. The junior must be 9 no later than August 31 of the current year. A junior golfer who enrolls in the program for the first time, must enroll in one of the beginner programs. Both beginner programs play on Tuesdays.

The beginners program provides instruction in rules and etiquette, chipping, putting and the full swing. One class day is spent on the golf course for 3-holes of play. The junior golfer spends only one summer in the beginners program and advances the next summer to play one of the three golf courses.

In the players program, the junior golfers play on the course every Monday. Scores are recorded to determine the junior's advancement in the program to reach the 18-hole level. Instruction in rules and etiquette and golf skills are also given every week.

Best of all, every year scholarships are awarded to golfers who have been involved in the Junior Golf program for at least four years. Scholarships can be as large as $10k! Click Here for scholarship details.

PGA Jr. League - Fun Youth Team Golf

Join the team. Play with friends. Learn the game with expert coaching! PGA Jr. League is bringing friends, families and communities together through golf. This league for boys and girls ages 9-13 takes a team approach to golf. Participants attend weekly practices, compete in matches against other golf courses, are provided a uniform and receive instructions from a PGA Professional. Auburn Hills and Tex Consolver are forming teams now! No experience in golf is required to join. League Ambassadors of PGA Jr. League include Lexi Thompson, Rickie Fowler, Michelle Wie, Alex Morgan, Stephen Curry, and Rory McEllroy.
